VISION IS KEY

"VISION IS KEY" stems from the personal evolution of growing into my name, Keynes, and adopting "Key" as both an alias and a principle for direction. The project frames vision not as abstract ambition, but as structured long-term thinking, the same mindset required in business strategy and analytics. Through a magazine-style visual narrative that contrasts formal structure with expressive identity, I explored how perception, positioning, and symbolism shape personal and professional brand. The result is a cohesive creative statement on how clarity of vision functions as a strategic advantage, reinforcing that intentional identity design and disciplined long-term planning unlock opportunity.
